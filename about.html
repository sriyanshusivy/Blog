Our story

This blog started because Cupcake kept saying “we should write this down” every time something nice happened, and Sutu kept saying “yeah, we should” and then not doing it. Eventually Cupcake got tired of waiting.

We didn’t set out to make something beautiful. We just wanted a place to keep things — the trips we take, the food we make, the conversations that feel important at 11pm. A little archive of us.

We write from Kharagpur, West Bengal, and occasionally from wherever our last bus ticket takes us. If you’re reading this — hello, and thank you for being here in our little corner of the internet.
