On finding a café that feels like ours

S
Written by Sutu
☕

Some places just feel like they were made for you. The right kind of light coming through the window, the right amount of noise, a corner table that always seems to be free. Sutu found this one first — on a Tuesday afternoon with nowhere to be — then made Cupcake come along the following weekend. That visit turned into three hours and two rounds of coffee.

“The right café is the one where you lose track of time without noticing.”

How to know when you’ve found it

You know a place is yours when you stop noticing it. When you don’t look around anymore because you already know what’s there. When you sit down and immediately feel your shoulders drop. That’s the test. We’ve been going back every week since.
